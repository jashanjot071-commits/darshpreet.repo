Meet the Creator

The Woman Behind the Details

Hi, I'm DK — a passionate nail artist based in Ludhiana, Punjab. What started as a hobby of painting my own nails grew into something I never imagined: a thriving nail brand that serves hundreds of women across India.

I've always believed that the smallest details can transform how you feel about yourself. That one perfect set of nails can give you the confidence to walk into any room. That's what Details by DK is about.

Every product we sell is something I've personally tested. Every design I create is crafted with hours of practice and genuine love for the art. This is not just a business — it's a calling.

— Dimple K.
